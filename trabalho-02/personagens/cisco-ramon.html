Cisco Ramon/Vibro

Francisco "Cisco" Ramon, apelidado de Vibro por Barry Allen, é um gênio da engenharia mecânica e uma meta-humano dos Laboratórios S.T.A.R., anteriormente servindo como guarda da sua prisão temporária. Ele veio de uma família pobre, de Porto Rico, e fornece muito alívio cômico no seu local de trabalho duro. Cisco também é um membro da Equipe Flash. Ele também era amigo de Harrison Wells antes de descobrir a sua verdadeira identidade como Eobard Thawne, embora Eobard visse Cisco como um filho adotivo antes e depois da descoberta. Ele também desenvolveu uma rivalidade com Hartley Rathaway e um relacionamento amigável com Ray Palmer. Mais tarde foi revelado que Cisco havia sido afetado pelo acelerador de partículas, fazendo com que ele se tornasse um meta-humano com a capacidade de ver através de vibrações e a capacidade de reter memórias de cronogramas alternativos de si mesmo antes de serem alterados.

Poderes

  • Precognition: Though Cisco was murdered by Eobard Thawne in an alternate timeline, he retains the memory of the murder. Eobard is impressed and realizes that Cisco may have been affected by the particle accelerator, as he can see through the vibrations of the universe and retain traces of alternate timelines. Cisco had dreams and visions envisioned his own death an alternate timeline made void by Barry's accidental time-traveling, and also had a vision of Atom-Smasher and Zoom speaking to each other in Earth 2 after seeing Atom-Smasher in person on Flash Day. These visions are typically hazy and dark, with a slightly distorted quality. Recently, he has been shown to get visions of events in his own world involving Earth 2's Sand Demon after touching his cells on a microscope slide, which he used to find where he was keeping Patty Spivot captive and what weapon he planned to use as a distraction. This suggests that Cisco's powers also allow him to see past events in his own timeline where he was not physically present as well. It has also been proven that Cisco can also see someone's past as shown with Kendra Saunders by making physical contact with that person. Somehow, he was able to use his powers to enable him to hit Zoom, which he lightly addressed in "Gorilla Warfare." In the episode "The Reverse-Flash Returns" it was revealed that his abilities are usually triggered by fear and adrenalin; and after he donned a modified visor engineered by Earth-2's Harrison Wells that triggers vibes easily and allows Cisco to control how long he can stay in a vibe, it was shown that he can also see the future as he saw Reverse-Flash kill Tina McGee.

Possíveis poderes

Sonic blasts: According to his Earth-2 counterpart, Cisco is able to generate blasts from his hands, in the form of sonic waves. Currently, he has yet to learn how to do this.
